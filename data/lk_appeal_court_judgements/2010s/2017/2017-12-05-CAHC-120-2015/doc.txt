Mr. Johnthasan, the Counsel for the Accused appellant is present in Court and

he tenders his apologies for not being present on the last occasion. He moves

Court that his client wants to withdraw this appeal. On the request of the

Counsel the Court to directly inquires from the Accused Appellant.

Since the Accused Appellant cannot speak in Sinh ala the Court communicate

with the Accused Appellant with the assistance of Senior State Counsel,

Mr. D. S. Soosaitas.

The Accused Appellant confirms and moves to withdraw the appeal.

Accordingly withdrawal of the appeal is allowed and the appeal is dismissed.

The Counsel moves Court that since the Accused was incarcerated from the

date of the conviction, to implement the Sentence from the date of conviction.

State has no objections. Since the Accused Appellant was incarcerated from the

date of conviction the Court directs the Prison Authorities to implement the

sentence from the date of conviction namely, 29.07.2015.

1

Registrar is hereby directed to return the case record to the Registrar of High

Court of Jaffna for implementation of the Sentence.

Appeal is dismissed.
