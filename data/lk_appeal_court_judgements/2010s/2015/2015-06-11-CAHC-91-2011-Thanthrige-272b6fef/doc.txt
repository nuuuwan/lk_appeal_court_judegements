VIJITH K. MALALGODA PCJ (PICA)

In this case the accused-appellant was indicted before the

High Court of Colombo on two counts under the Poisons Opium and

Dangerous Drugs Ordinance for having being in possession and

trafficking of quantity less than 1 gram of heroin. After full trial, the

accused-appellant was convicted by the learned High Court Judge and

imposed 7 years rigorous imprisonment on both counts with a fine of

Rs. 50,000/- and in default a period of 01 year rigorous imprisonment.

The above two sentences were ordered to run consecutively and the

learned High Court Judge in his order had given reasons as to why he

decided to impose the maximum and ordered them to run

consecutively. The accused-appellant has committed this offence whilst

3

consecutively after the operation of the main sentence. Subject to the

above variation in the sentences the appeal stands dismissed.

At this stage the Counsel makes an application with regard

to the operation of the sentences. Court makes order that the operation

of the sentences to commence from the date of conviction i.e. 24-05-

2011.

The Registrar is directed to forward this case record together

with this order to the Registrar of the High Court of Colombo in order to

implement this sentence.

H.C.J. MADAWALA, J.

I agree.
