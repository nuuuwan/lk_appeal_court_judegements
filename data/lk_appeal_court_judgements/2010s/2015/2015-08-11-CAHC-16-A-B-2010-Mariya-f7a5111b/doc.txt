This is a matter already concluded by this Court by

confirming the sentence of the learned High Court Judge. However

counsel for the Appellant moved this court to make an order to

operate the sentence from the date of conviction, when the

judgment was pronounced. This matter is coming up today to

consider the above request by the counsel.

has no objection for the above application.
