K. K. Wickremasinghe, J.

It is being informed that the State Counsel is conceding

the application for bail in this matter and therefore we decide to

enlarge the suspect on bail on the following conditions:

1. A surety bail of Rs. 100,000/- each with two sureties acceptable to

the Magistrate, Mt. Lavinia.

2. The Suspect-Petitioner is ordered to report to the Police Narcotic

Bureau on the 1st Sunday of each month between 8.30 a.m. to 12.00

noon.

3. The suspect-Petitioner is warned not to interfere with the

prosecution witnesses on investigation.
